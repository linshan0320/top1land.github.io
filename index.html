SEE HOW OUR PROCESS WORKS

No Fees. No Commissions. Just Cash In Your Pocket.

No listing fees. No gimmicks. We start by gathering your property information including the reference ID or the parcel ID, so we can do our due diligence on your land property. We will make you a cash offer, and you decide if we’re a fit for you or not. Our land buying process is truly as simple as that.

1. Contact Us

Once you provide us with a few basic pieces of information, using our simple and easy to use Sell My Land Form, we will automatically receive a copy of that information and begin our review process. Using a mixture of county assessment information, recent local sales data, and research on comparable land properties in the area, we will determine what we’d be able to offer you for your land.

2. Professional Consultation

A customer care representative will call back to discuss the details of your land. We will then provide you with ideal solutions on how to gain the best cash offer for your land.

3. Get a Cash Offer Estimate

After the consultation, we are going to visit your land so that we can craft the best sales strategy for your land in your presence. Such visits make it easy for us to provide you with an idealist solution to sell your land as is. Most of the times we make cash offers on the spot after having a look at the land.

4. Sell Your Land for CASH

After accepting our offer, all parties involved sign a contractual agreement after which full payments are done in 30 days or less. And unlike other companies which make you an offer and disappear, we will keep in touch with you on a weekly basis.
